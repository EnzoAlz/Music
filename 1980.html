She Blinded Me With Science

The song She Blinded Me With Science by Thomas Dolby is an experience of a song. It has a funky beat and random sounds that come together to make a new kind of music. The random interjection of the vaugly European science man of the blinding nature of science tickles my heart and ears.

Rise

The song Rise by Herb Alpert is a funky jazz song. It has little lyrics. Like other songs I found this from the movie "Spiderhead" on netflix (a good watch) and loved it. The trumpet sounds amazing and is easily the center of this song. It is like a constant beat drop that slowly moves into another beat drop. It is easily a 5/5 song and I recomend it.
